Tithorea

Residence

Welcome to an elegant room where natural simplicity meets comfort and inspiration. Interior details emphasize the concept of eco-design and comfort, and a spacious window opens up to a view of nature.

In our complex you will find apartments with stone finishing, a bathroom and a balcony, which can accommodate from 2 to 6 people.

Comfort & Design

Each apartment is carefully designed to provide maximum comfort while maintaining ecological principles. Natural materials, energy-efficient solutions, and breathtaking views create a unique atmosphere.

Family Friendly

Perfect for families with children. Spacious rooms, safe environment, and special amenities for kids make your stay comfortable and memorable for the whole family.

Parnassos

The oldest historical mention of Tithorea is made by Herodotus, who, describing Xerxes' march towards Athens after the Battle of Thermopylae in 480 BC, writes that the Persian soldiers burned the cities of the Cephissus valley.

Natural Beauty

Discover the breathtaking landscapes of Mount Parnassos, with its lush forests, crystal-clear streams, and diverse wildlife. Perfect for hiking, photography, and reconnecting with nature.

Cultural Heritage

Explore ancient ruins, traditional villages, and local museums that tell the rich history of this magnificent region. Experience the authentic Greek culture and hospitality.
